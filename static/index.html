About BrainAge

The Science

"Brain Age" is a biomarker derived from neuroimaging data. The difference between a patient's predicted brain age and their actual chronological age—known as the Brain Age Gap (BAG)—can indicate accelerated aging or resilience against neurodegenerative diseases like Alzheimer's.

The Architecture

  • T2 Model

    A specialized ResNet50 regressor trained solely on T2-weighted structural MRI scans for rapid, single-modality inference.

  • Fusion Model

    A Late Fusion architecture that processes T1, T2, PD, and MRA modalities through independent feature extractors before aggregating them for a holistic prediction.
